Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program suitable for non-CS students?

Absolutely! We start from the very basics (Hello World) and gradually move to advanced concepts. 40% of our successful placements are from non-IT backgrounds like Mechanical and Civil engineering.

Do you provide placement guarantee?

We provide 100% placement assistance. This means we help you with resumes, mock interviews, and refer you to our network of 50+ hiring partners until you get placed. Your success is our success.

How are the classes conducted?

Classes are conducted live online (Zoom/Google Meet) on weekends and weekday evenings to accommodate both students and working professionals. Recordings are provided if you miss a session.

What if I get stuck while coding?

We have a dedicated discord community and teaching assistants available 12 hours a day to help you debug your code and unblock you instantly.
